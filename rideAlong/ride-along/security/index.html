Your Safety is our Priority

Our priority is to ensure that you can move around freely, efficiently and stay connected with people and places that matter to you. Therefore, we have a strong commitment to safety, which includes establishing new safety standards and utilizing technology to minimize the occurrence of incidents.

There’s emergency help if you need it

There’s emergency help if you need it

In an emergency, speed and accuracy are critical. If you call 911 or your emergency contact from the Ridealong app, the app displays your live location and trip details, so you can share them with the emergency dispatcher.

We verify our members.

We require all our members to verify their identification using government-issued IDs. In particular, driver's licenses are mandatory for submission. Please be assured that we take the privacy of all identification information very seriously and ensure that it is kept confidential.

Masks are no longer Mandatory

According to a recent announcement, masks are no longer mandatory for riders and drivers using RideAlong. However, the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) still recommends wearing a mask in certain situations. It is important to be considerate of others who may prefer to wear a mask due to personal or health reasons, and you can always cancel a trip if you feel uncomfortable.

A respectful community

We always treat each other with respect, and we don’t tolerate discrimination. Anyone who violates our policies may be permanently removed from the platform. You can cancel any ride that makes you feel unsafe.
